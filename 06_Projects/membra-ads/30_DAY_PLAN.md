# Membra Ads — 30-Day Launch Plan

Assumption: 4 focused hours per day. Zero budget. Realistic for manual-first, automate-later.

## Week 1: Foundation + Vendor Research

| Day | Hour 1 | Hour 2 | Hour 3 | Hour 4 |
|-----|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Define exact offer: "7-day local ad campaign with windows, cars, wear." | Pick one launch neighborhood. | Pick one advertiser niche (pizza, gyms, barbers, nightlife). | Create simple lead/owner tracking sheet. |
| 2 | Write owner promise: earn from window, car, clothing. | Write advertiser promise: verified local reach. | Define three products: Window Kit, Car Kit, Wear Kit. | Define proof rules for each product. |
| 3 | Create Membra campaign ID format. | Create owner ID and asset ID format. | Create placement ID and QR ID format. | Create first campaign proof checklist. |
| 4 | Contact Printful via API/partnership/sales. | Contact Printify via API/partnership/sales. | Contact Gelato via sales/API. | Record pricing, minimums, sample costs, API availability. |
| 5 | Contact Sticker Mule for decals/magnets. | Contact two local sign shops. | Ask about removable window clings and car magnets. | Ask about variable QR printing. |
| 6 | Contact GoToTags for custom NFC stickers. | Contact Shop NFC / NFC Tagify as backup. | Ask about printed QR + NFC encoding. | Ask about minimum order, unique URLs, shipping time. |
| 7 | Build simple landing page outline. | Write owner signup questions. | Write advertiser campaign request questions. | Write terms-style rules in plain English. |

| Day | Hour 1 | Hour 2 | Hour 3 | Hour 4 |

| 13 | Find 25 local advertisers. | Score them by fit. | Pick top 10. | Write custom pitch for each. |
| 14 | Contact 10 advertisers manually. | Offer a pilot campaign. | Offer free mockup. | Log replies and objections. |

## Week 3: Close First Advertiser + Production

| Day | Hour 1 | Hour 2 | Hour 3 | Hour 4 |
|-----|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| 15 | Follow up with non-responders. | Contact 10 more advertisers. | Improve pitch based on objections. | Create two more campaign mockups. |
| 16 | Choose first serious advertiser. | Define campaign radius and duration. | Define campaign inventory mix. | Estimate production cost and owner payouts. |
| 17 | Ask printer vendors for quote on real campaign kit. | Ask NFC vendor for quote on optional tags. | Choose cheapest acceptable QR-only path. | Prepare invoice/payment request. |
| 18 | Finalize advertiser creative. | Create final templates. | Generate unique QR tracking links manually. | Build proof report template. |
| 19 | Get advertiser approval. | Collect advertiser payment or deposit. | Place print order only after payment. | Send shipping addresses to vendor or prepare manual shipments. |
| 20 | Notify owners selected for campaign. | Send campaign rules. | Confirm availability and consent. | Confirm payout amount and proof requirements. |
| 21 | Track shipping. | Prepare owner installation instructions. | Prepare proof photo examples. | Prepare failed-proof rules. |

## Week 4: Campaign Live + Proof + Payout

| Day | Hour 1 | Hour 2 | Hour 3 | Hour 4 |
|-----|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| 22 | Owners receive kits. | Confirm receipt. | Owners install/wear items. | Collect first proof photos. |
| 23 | Validate proof manually. | Check photo quality. | Check time and location. | Mark placements active or rejected. |
| 24 | Campaign live day 1. | Collect proof batch 1. | Track QR scans. | Send advertiser first proof update. |
| 25 | Campaign live day 2. | Fix bad placements. | Replace missing proof owners if needed. | Update analytics sheet. |
| 26 | Campaign live day 3–4. | Collect proof. | Track scans. | Prepare midpoint report. |
| 27 | Campaign live day 5. | Contact advertiser about extending. | Ask owners for testimonials. | Identify top-performing placements. |
| 28 | Campaign live day 6–7. | Final proof collection. | Final scan/tap totals. | Calculate owner payouts. |
| 29 | Release payouts manually. | Send advertiser proof report. | Ask for testimonial. | Pitch second campaign. |
| 30 | Review what broke. | Decide what to automate first. | Choose backend MVP features. | Build next 30-day roadmap. |

## First Paid Package

- **Price**: $250–$500 pilot campaign
- **Duration**: Seven days
- **Placements**: 5–10 verified placements
- **Mix**: Window, car, and/or wear
- **Tracking**: QR tracking
- **Proof**: Daily proof photos
- **Report**: Final proof report

## Owner Payout Tiers

- **Windows**: $5–$20/day
- **Cars**: $3–$25/day
- **Wear**: $5–$50/day

## Operational Rules

1. Do not print anything until the advertiser pays.
2. Do not pay owners until proof is verified.
3. Do not let QR/NFC bypass Membra.
4. Do not build a full app until the manual campaign works.

## First Automation Priority (Post-30-Day)

1. Owner signup + asset listing (web form)
2. Advertiser campaign builder (web form)
3. Creative review workflow (upload → approve/reject)
4. QR generation + tracking URL creation
5. Payment intent creation (Stripe)
6. Proof photo upload + GPS capture
7. Scan event logging + basic analytics
8. Payout release via Stripe Connect transfer
