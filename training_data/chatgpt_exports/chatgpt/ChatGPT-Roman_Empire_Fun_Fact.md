# Roman Empire Fun Fact

#### You:
Tell me a random fun fact about the Roman Empire

#### ChatGPT:
Roman concrete could actually harden over time in seawater, which is one reason some Roman harbors and breakwaters have lasted for about 2,000 years.

The neat part is that seawater reacted with volcanic ash in the concrete and helped grow minerals that strengthened it, instead of slowly destroying it the way saltwater usually damages modern concrete.
