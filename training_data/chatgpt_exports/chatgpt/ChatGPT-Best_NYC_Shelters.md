## 🧠 What “best” actually means in NYC (real talk)

NYC shelters vary a LOT. The “best” ones usually have:

- **Smaller size (Safe Haven > large intake)**
- **Nonprofit operators (CAMBA, Project Renewal, etc.)**
- **Private/semi-private rooms**
- **On-site services (case workers, job help, housing placement)**

Big intake shelters = necessary, but not comfortable.\
Goal = get placed → then transferred to something better.
